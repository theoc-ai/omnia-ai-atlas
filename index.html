How to score

For each sub-domain, rate your Current state — how AI is used today — and your Target — the ambition you're aiming for. Both use the same 0–5 maturity scale below.

0
No AI
No use of AI; the capability runs entirely on manual or rules-based processes.
1
Exploring
Experimenting with AI in isolated pilots or proofs of concept.
2
Assisting
AI supports a few discrete tasks but isn't part of the core workflow.
3
Bolted on
AI bolted into select parts of the process to speed up existing steps.
4
Embedded
AI embedded across most of the process and trusted in day-to-day operations.
5
Redesigned
The capability is redesigned around AI, not just augmented by it.
